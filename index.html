Debuted on May 26, 2015, here are few of the achievements of SEVENTEEN

    International Achievements

  • MTV Music Video Awards - nominated for Best K-pop and Best New Artist (2021-2022)
  • Billboard Music Awards - nominated for Top Social Artist (2021)
  • QQ Music 'Boom Boom Awards' (China) - won Asian Pioneer Idol Group of The Year (2020)
  • Asia Music Festival (China) - won Album of the Year and Asia's Group of the Year (2019)
  • MTV Europe Music Awards - nominated for Best Korean Act (2017),
  • Teen Choice Awards - nominated for Choice International Artist (2017)

    • National Achievements

  • 2020 Korean Popular Culture and Arts Awards - received the Prime Minister's Commendation
  • 2021 Newsis Hallyu Expo - received the Minister of Culture, Sports and Tourism Award
  • Asia Artist Awards - won Album of the Year (2019) and Artist of the Year (2021)
  • Gaon Chart Music Awards - won Hot Performance Artist (2017) and a consistent nominee for Album of the Year
  • Golden Disc Awards - won Best New Artist (2016) and consistent recepient of the Disc Bonsang Award
  • Melon Music Awards - won Stage of the Year (2019)
  • Mnet Asian Music Awards - won Global Favourite Performer (2020), Notable Achievement Artist (2020), Best OST (2018), Best Dance Performance (2017-2018), Best World Performer (2016) and a consistent Worldwide Fans' Choice
  • Seoul Music Awards - won New Artist Award (2016) and a consistent recepient of the Bonsang Award
